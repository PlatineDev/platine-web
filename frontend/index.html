// real vs expected

Not just data.
A repair plan.

Every component is compared against its expected values. Platine tells you what's wrong, how bad it is, and what to do — in order of priority.

No database of manufacturers needed — universal hardware thresholds cover 95% of all issues. SMART sectors should be 0. CPU should run at 100%. Battery should be above 80%. Platine knows this so you don't have to.
